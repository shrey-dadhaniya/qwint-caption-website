Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Does it work with Premiere Pro 2023 and 2024? +

Yes! Qwint Caption is fully compatible with Adobe Premiere Pro 2022, 2023, and 2024 (versions 22.0 and above). We regularly update the plugin to support the latest Premiere Pro releases, so you'll always be covered.

How accurate are the AI-generated captions? +

Qwint Caption uses state-of-the-art AI transcription that achieves 95-98% word accuracy on clear audio. Accuracy depends on audio quality, background noise, and speaker clarity. We recommend recording in a quiet environment for best results. You can always fine-tune captions directly in Premiere Pro after generation.

Which languages are supported? +

We support 50+ languages including English, Hindi, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, Arabic, Tamil, Telugu, and many more. The plugin auto-detects the language spoken in your video, or you can manually select a language for best accuracy.

How does the credit system work? +

Credits are measured in minutes of video processed. For example, generating captions for a 5-minute video uses 5 minutes of credits. You get 1 minute free on signup. After that, you can purchase credit packs starting at ₹199. Credits never expire, so you can use them at your own pace.

What caption formats can I export? +

Qwint Caption places captions directly on your Premiere Pro timeline as editable text layers. You can also export as SRT (SubRip), VTT (WebVTT), or bake captions into your video during export — whichever workflow suits you best.

Is my video data secure? +

Absolutely. Your video audio is processed securely and is never stored or used for training purposes. All data is encrypted in transit. We take your privacy seriously — see our Privacy Policy for full details.
